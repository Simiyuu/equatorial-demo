Our Story

A Journey Through East African Cuisine

Equatorial was born from a simple belief — that the flavours of East Africa deserve a world-class stage. From the spice routes of the Swahili Coast to the highland farms of the Rift Valley, every ingredient we use tells the story of this extraordinary region.

Our head chef brings over fifteen years of experience across Nairobi, London, and Dubai, returning home with a vision: to honour tradition while pushing the boundaries of what African fine dining can be.
